Rent an Aircraft

We have several types of aircraft for you to choose from depending on your needs. We cater for short to mid range flying sorties, with aircraft ranges from 400-1000 miles.

Experience Flights

Never flown in a light aircraft before? No Problem. We have experienced instructors on hand with state of the art aircraft waiting to give you your first thrill of flying!

Airshows and Meets

We also organise small airshows and 'fly-in' meets, as well as organising groups to visit larger airshows both nationally and around Europe.

Where We Are

Situated on the beautiful Sussex coast, and with good links to France and the channel islands, Shoreham (Brighton) is the perfect start to any flight.
