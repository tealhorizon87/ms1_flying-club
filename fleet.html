The Fleet

Cessna 172

The Cessna 172 is the ultimate recreational flyer. A high wing position makes it the perfect choice for aerial sight-seeing. Our 172's are all 4 seaters (1 crew and 3 passengers) with seperate luggage compartment, and a maximum range of 650 nautical miles.

Price: £145 per hobbs hour - fuel and landing fees not included.

Piper Warrior

Versatile and compact, the PA-28 Piper Warrior is designed for training, air taxi and recreational use. Our version of this classic is a 4 seater (1 crew and 3 passengers) with seperate luggage compartment, a glass-cockpit style Garmin 1000 instrument system and a maximum range of 450 nautical miles.

Price: £150 per hobbs hour - fuel and landing fees not included.

Piper Seneca

The PA-34 Piper Seneca is a great light twin. With more space and higher speeds, your plans for longer trips may now be a reality. All of our Senecas are 6 seater (1 crew and 5 passengers) with seperate luggage compartment, a glass-cockpit style Garmin 1000 instrument system and a maximum range of 850 nautical miles.

Price: £375 per hobbs hour - fuel and landing fees not included.

Experience Flights

Do you want to know what it feels like to fly a plane? Or maybe you've always wanted to see your house from above? With our experience flights, you can do this and more. From local sight-seeing and thrilling aerobatic flights to helping with flying phobias, our professional instructors are here to help.

What's Included

You will start with a briefing from your flight instructor/pilot. If you haven't already, this is the time to tell our staff what you want to get out of your flight. Your pilot will then take you out to our state of the art Diamond da40 and get you settled in the cockpit. Flights last approximately 1 hour from take-off to landing, and there will be the opportunity to get a video of your flight at the end.

Price

1 flight: £200
5 flights: £750
in-flight recording: £45
